About Everyman Bristol

Welcome to Everyman Bristol, housed in the beautifully restored Whiteladies Picture House and first opened in 1921, it is a captivating blend of historic art deco charm and modern luxury nestled on Whiteladies Road in Clifton. With three cinema screens, all fitted with our renowned everyman sofas and armchair seating, and hosting a chic bar and lounge. You can enjoy our range of gourmet food and drinks options including sharing plates, handcrafted burgers, desserts, freshly crafted cocktails, wines, beers, & more. Dine the lounge before your film, or have them delivered directly to your seat, creating seamless & luxurious relaxation.

Offering Silver Screen, National Theatre Live, Baby Club and many more cinema experiences so everyone can enjoy the magic of cinema. You can browse the latest film listings and showtimes in Bristol and book tickets online in advance for the best seat in the house.

Looking to host a special event? Find out more about our private hire options at Everyman Bristol - perfect for special celebrations, corporate presentations, or private movie screenings. We are open 10am daily (or half an hour before the first film of the day, if earlier). Find more details about parking, accessibility, screen information and more from the drop down list below.

Parking

On-street parking is available on Melrose Place and Alma Road adjacent to the cinema.

Multi-storey parking is available at Clifton Down Shopping Centre, open until 22:30.

Disabled access

Everyman Bristol has ground floor access to all auditoria, with disabled seats A7-9 in Screen 1, G1 in Screen 2 and H1-2 in Screen 3.

The step-free entrance is on the side of the building on Melrose Place. Please ring the bell on the door and a team member will let you in.

Opening times

10am daily (or half an hour before the first film of the day, if earlier).
